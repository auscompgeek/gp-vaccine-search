GPVaccineSearch.com

Unofficial Australian GP AstraZeneca Search

Searches HotDoc for nearby GPs, and ranks them by their next available appointment for first AstraZeneca dose.

More Resources

  • Manually search next appointments on HotDoc
  • Official Aus Gov Eligibility Checker
  • Unofficial Pfizer appointment finder: https://covidqueue.com/

Click 'Get My Location' to find neaby GPs.

Privacy note: Your location is not logged or stored. HotDoc receives your location; their privacy policy is here. This website is open source on Github, contributions welcome :)
